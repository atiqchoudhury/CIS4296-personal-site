About Me

I am currently a Senior majoring in Information Science & Technology at Temple University.

Projects

Here are some personal projects and projects that I have worked on during my coursework.
Ro-Bangladesh

A model country based on the country of Bangladesh hosted on the ROBLOX platform. We strive to bring a unique experience to everyone who participates in our group to experience the culture of Bangladesh and interact with others to explore the wonders of Bangladesh in a virtual setting. We provide realistic approaches to different roleplay aspects, such as politics, civil services, military, and many more.

Places to Eat!

A custom-made HTML website incorporating elements such as CSS, JSON, AJAX, REACT, and Javascript. This website is attributed to one of my friends, Brian Tran, who made a food recommendation list around the city of Philadelphia. This website categorizes the recommendations based on which part of the world the restuarant background.
